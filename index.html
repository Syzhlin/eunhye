📐 문법 포인트 — since vs for 실전 연습
SINCE — 특정 시점부터
since 7:45 · since Monday
since I was young
"언제부터?"에 대한 답
시각 / 요일 / 날짜 / 사건
FOR — 기간 동안
for an hour · for 3 months
for a long time
"얼마 동안?"에 대한 답
숫자 + 시간 단위
✏️ 빈칸에 since 또는 for를 써봐요 — 5문제
Q1
나는 어제부터 아무것도 못 먹었어.
I haven't eaten anything yesterday.
힌트: '어제'는 특정 날짜예요.
Q2
나 지금 세 시간째 여기서 기다리고 있어.
I've been waiting here three hours.
힌트: '세 시간'은 기간이에요.
Q3 ⚠️ 가장 헷갈리는 문제!
우리 마지막으로 만난 지 5년 됐어.
It's been five years since we last met.
힌트: "5년" 자체는 기간, "since we last met"은 따로 있어요. 빈칸엔 "5년"에 맞는 것!
Q4
나는 아침부터 커피를 한 잔도 안 마셨어.
I haven't had any coffee this morning.
힌트: 'this morning'은 특정 시점이에요.
Q5
우리 한 달째 못 봤잖아!
We haven't seen each other a month!
힌트: '한 달'은 기간이에요. "~째"는 영어로?

SPEAKING · FLUENCY TRAINING
내 이야기로 말해봐요 — 길고 자연스럽게
💡 한 문장으로 끝내지 말기! 아래 연결어와 감정 표현을 써서 3–4문장 이상 말해봐요. 모델 답변을 참고해서 나만의 이야기로 바꾸면 돼요.
Q1
누군가를 오래 기다려본 적 있어요? 몇 시부터였어요?
📌 핵심 표현 프레임
I've been [waiting / here / sitting there] since ___.
I waited for ___ [minutes / hours] and they finally ___.
By the time ___ came, I had already ___.
💬 자연스럽게 말하는 방법 — 이 표현들을 섞어 써봐요!
시작하기
Oh, totally! Actually, yes! You know what... This happened to me once.
연결하고 이어가기
And then... At some point... By the time... Eventually...
감정 추가하기
I was so annoyed. I couldn't believe it. I was about to leave. I was pretty frustrated.
결론 짓기
In the end... Anyway... Long story short...
📖 모델 답변 (참고만 — 나만의 버전으로 바꿔요!)
"Oh, totally! This happened to me once. I was waiting for a friend at a cafe. I was there since 6 o'clock, but she didn't show up. At some point I checked my phone, but there was no message. I was about to leave when she finally texted me. In the end she came at 7:30, so I waited for over an hour. I was so annoyed, but she apologized a lot so I let it go."
✏️ 나의 답변 메모
Q2
약속에 늦어본 적 있어요? 어떻게 설명했어요?
📌 핵심 표현 프레임
There was a problem with ___ [the bus / traffic / my phone...].
I ran / rushed from ___ [the station / the office / home].
I couldn't get a signal. / My phone died. / There was heavy traffic.
💬 자연스럽게 말하는 방법
상황 설명하기
So basically... What happened was... I was supposed to...
핑계 표현
There was a problem with ~ I missed the bus. I got stuck in traffic. My phone was dead.
상대 반응 말하기
She/He was upset. They said "You're always late!" Luckily, they understood.
나의 감정
I felt so bad. I was so stressed. I ran as fast as I could.
📖 모델 답변
"Yes! What happened was, I was supposed to meet a friend for lunch. But there was a problem with the subway — it stopped for like 20 minutes. I was so stressed. I tried to call her, but I couldn't get a signal. I ran from the station and finally got there. She was a bit upset, but when I explained, luckily she understood."
✏️ 나의 답변 메모
Q3
말하다가 "아, 그 말 하지 말았어야 했는데" 싶은 적 있어요?
📌 핵심 표현 프레임
I didn't mean to ___ [hurt them / say that / bring it up].
That came out wrong. (말이 이상하게 나왔어)
I shouldn't have said that. (그 말 하지 말았어야 했는데)
💬 자연스럽게 말하는 방법
상황 설명
I was just trying to... I didn't realize that... It just slipped out.
사과 / 해명
I didn't mean to ~ That came out wrong. I feel so bad about it. I apologized right away.
상대방 반응
They seemed hurt. She/He looked surprised. They forgave me eventually.
교훈
I've been more careful since. I learned my lesson. I think before I speak now.
📖 모델 답변
"Hmm, yes actually. Once I was just trying to give my friend some honest feedback on her outfit, but... it just slipped out in the worst way. I didn't mean to hurt her feelings at all. That came out totally wrong. She seemed really hurt, so I apologized right away. I feel so bad when I think about it now. I've been more careful since then."
✏️ 나의 답변 메모

WRITING · PRACTICE
나만의 문장 만들기 — 단계별 심화
① 표현별 3단계 연습 — since / I didn't mean to / The only ~ is ~
── since ──────────────────────────────
LV1 빈칸 채우기
나는 대학교 때부터 커피를 마셔왔어.
I've been drinking coffee college.
나는 어젯밤부터 머리가 아팠어.
I've had a headache last night.
LV2 한국어 보고 영어로
나는 처음 만난 날부터 그 사람이 좋았어.
힌트: I've liked ___ since the day we ___.
LV3 자유 작문 — since를 써서 나만의 문장!
── I didn't mean to ~ ───────────────────
LV1 빈칸 채우기
나는 그의 감정을 상하게 하려고 한 게 아니었어.
I didn't mean to his feelings.
나는 그렇게 말하려고 한 게 아니었어.
I didn't mean to that.
LV2 한국어 보고 영어로
나는 그 약속을 취소하려고 한 게 아니었어. 그냥 너무 바빴어.
힌트: I didn't mean to ___ the plan. I was just ___.
LV3 자유 작문
── The only ~ is ~ (강조 구조) ────────────
LV1 빈칸 채우기
내가 원하는 것은 오직 커피 한 잔뿐이야.
The only I want is .
내가 필요한 것은 오직 휴식뿐이야.
The only I need is .
LV2 한국어 보고 영어로
내가 걱정하는 사람은 오직 너뿐이야.
힌트: The only person I ___ about is ___.
LV3 자유 작문
② 미니 스토리 만들기 — 오늘 씬처럼 상황 써봐요
📖 상황 설명
친구와 카페에서 만나기로 했는데, 약속에 늦었어요. 버스에 문제가 있었고, 전화도 안 됐어요. 결국 뛰어서 도착했고, 친구는 많이 기다렸어요. 이 상황을 4–5문장으로 영어로 써봐요.
꼭 써야 할 표현: There was a problem with ~ · I ran · I've been here since · I'm so sorry
③ 한→영 변환 — 통째로 써봐요
① 나는 중학교 때부터 영어를 좋아했어. 그래서 요즘도 계속 공부하고 있어.
② 그럴 의도가 아니었어. 그냥 솔직하게 말하려고 했는데 말이 이상하게 나온 것 같아. 미안해.
③ 내가 지금 관심 있는 건 오직 영어뿐이야. 이제 거의 6개월째 공부하고 있어!
